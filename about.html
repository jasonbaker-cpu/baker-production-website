About

Perth Property Media, Done Right.

Baker Production is a small Perth-based studio producing photography, video, drone, floor plans, and virtual editing for property listings. We work mostly with real estate agents across the metro area, plus the occasional homeowner who wants their place shot properly before it hits the market.

What we actually do

Everything a listing needs, in one place. Photography, twilight shoots, drone, day-to-dusk conversions, walkthrough video, social reels, 2D and 3D floor plans, and virtual staging. Most studios do one or two of these well. We do all of them, every shoot, fast.

The team

There's a small group of us — three part-time photographers (plus me, Jason, still shooting most days), a coordinator handling the scheduling and admin side, and an editor working through the back end so galleries land in your inbox quickly.

Keeping the team tight means every shoot looks consistent. You're not getting a different style every time you book.

What makes us different

Speed. We turn galleries around fast — usually next-day delivery. In a market where listings go live in 48 hours, that matters more than people pretend it doesn't.

Quality. Every property gets full coverage — the wide angles, the natural light, the exterior shot at the right time of day. We've shot enough Perth properties to know what sells them.

Easy to deal with. Online booking, instant confirmation, no chasing. Your photographer turns up on time with everything sorted. No calls asking where to park.

Local. We're in Perth, we know Perth, we know what works for Perth properties. Subiaco is not the Hills is not South Perth.

Where we work

Perth metro and surrounds — from Alkimos to Rockingham, the coast to the Hills. If you're not sure whether your patch is in our area, just ask.
